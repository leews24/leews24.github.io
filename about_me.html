Warren Lee

Warren is a student at the University of Michigan where he is studying UX Design. He began making videos in high school, where one assignment involved him making a music video. Currently, he produces videos for SHEI Magazine as a part of their video team as well as help his PR team create promotional videos. He also plans to produce videos for personal use, although he has never gone around to actually doing it.

In his free time, Warren likes to make electronic music, eat chocolate, or play Pokemon.
