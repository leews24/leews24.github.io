Sinaboro 17th Concert PV
March 2017


Filmed by Warren Lee
Edited by Warren Lee
Music by Heize

Equipment Canon EOS Rebel T6i and GoPro Hero 3+
Edited on Adobe Premiere Pro CC 2015

Produced by Sinaboro
Sinaboro 17th Annual Concert: Peculiar Paradise

Production Notes

The theme of Sinaboro’s 17th Concert was Peculiar Paradise, somewhat close to a beach/vacation theme. As such, the main promo video tried to reflect that by creating an upbeat, exciting tone. I used Heize’s upbeat house song as well as employed the white letterboxes to achieve this.

I also feel like this is one of the few videos where I was able to use stock footage naturally. The beach and sea footage blend in seamlessly with the rest of the content.
