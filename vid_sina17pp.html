Peculiar Paradise
March 2017


Directed by Warren Lee
Written by Warren Lee
Produced by Warren Lee and Jenny Lee

Filmed by Warren Lee
Edited by Warren Lee
Music by Satoru Kousaki, Beenzino, Keith Ape, Zico, and Jisoo Lee

Equipment: Canon EOS Rebel T6i, Panasonic AC160A, and GoPro Hero 3+
Edited on Adobe Premiere Pro CC 2015 and After Effect CC 2015

Production Notes

The longest video I’ve worked on was this one. From the script to the post-edits, I managed every part of the video except subtitles (courtesy of my friend) and the music. The video was originally split into multiple parts to be shown in between acts of the Sinaboro Concert.

I do not have lots experience with videos with narratives and this was definitely a learning experience. I was also able to gain a lot of knowledge in equipment control between shoot sessions as well as communicating with a large amount of cast.
