Production Notes

This is one of the few shoots where I did not attend the filming process at all. There was some minor communication problems with the filmographer which led to a large selection of too short, shaky, or unfocused footage.

I tried to solve this by editing the footage to a fast tempo, constantly cutting in and out of angles of minor difference or different aspect ratios. I decided to experiment further and play around with much of the aspect ratio, even going as far to replicate a VHS tone in certain cuts.
